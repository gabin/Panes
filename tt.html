What Next?
Your Digital ID was delivered to your Web browser. To ensure your ID was properly installed, follow the instructions below:

For Netscape (Communicator):
  1. Click on the Security icon on the toolbar, or select the Communicator menu, Security Info option.
  2. In the Security Info window, click the Yours option in the left hand frame.
    Your Digital ID should appear in the Certificates list.
  3. Select your Digital ID and click the View button.
For Netscape Navigator 3: It is recommended that you upgrade to a later version of Netscape. However, if you want to continue to use Netscape 3.0, follow these steps:
  1. Select Security Preferences from the Options menu.
  2. Select the Personal Certificates tab.
  3. Your new Digital ID should appear in the Personal Certificates list. It should be listed by the certificate name that you specified when you received your Digital ID.
    Note: If you did not enter a certificate name, try clicking on the white space at the top of the box. It will highlight, allowing you to enter a name.
  4. View your Digital ID by selecting it and then selecting the More Info button.

Consult our Help Desk and Tutorials:

  1. Go to the Help Desk to view our tutorials and other useful information.
  2. Go to the Digital ID Center to find out more about Digital IDs and Digital ID services.
